What is this?

This booklet will help you look back over the past year and to plan the year ahead of you.

Why is this good for me?

Planning your year is a good habit. It helps you identify your successes and sorrows and will help you realize how much can happen just in a year. By learning from the past you can plan your future so that you don't repeat the same patterns and can feel in control of your own life.

What do I need?

1st version (lone wolf version)

2nd version (group version)

Arrive. Put on some relaxing music. Pour yourself a hot beverage. Let go all of your expectations. Start when ready.
